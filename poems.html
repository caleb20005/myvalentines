Poems for You

Written from the heart, Pookie

A Friend Like You

In your laughter, I find joy, In your kindness, I find peace, You're a treasure beyond measure, And our friendship will never cease. Your heart so warm and true, Brightens every single day, With you by my side, I'm blessed, In every possible way.

Grateful for You

Words can't express what you mean, The joy you bring into my life, Through laughter and through tears, You've been there through it all. A friend like you is rare, A gift I cherish more each day, Thank you for being you, In every single way.

Always There

When skies are gray and days are long, You bring back hope, you bring back song. You stand beside me, calm and true, Life feels more gentle because of you.

Your Kindness

Your kindness moves in quiet ways, Like sunlight through the coldest days. No grand display, no loud acclaim, Just steady love that stays the same.

One of a Kind

Some hearts are rare, both brave and bright, They lift the dark, they hold the light. In every room, in every view, I am grateful life gave me you.

Simple Joy

A laugh we share, a little talk, A memory made on an evening walk. The sweetest gifts are often small, Yet friendship makes them best of all.

Safe Place

In storms of doubt, in restless night, Your words can set my spirit right. You are the calm, the steady shore, The kind of friend I asked for more.

Grateful Heart

I count my blessings one by one, And there you are with morning sun. A loyal heart, a voice so true, I carry endless thanks for you.

Golden Bond

Not every tie is made to last, But ours keeps growing, deep and vast. Through every season, old and new, This golden bond stays strong and true.

Brightest Days

You turn the ordinary bright, You make the heavy spirit light. With every smile and word you say, You color all my darkest gray.

Near or Far

Though miles can stretch and clocks can race, Friendship still keeps its steady place. Near or far, both old and new, My heart still finds its way to you.

Quiet Strength

You do not need a crown or stage, Your gentle strength outlives an age. In silent ways you help me stand, With patient heart and open hand.

Cherished Friend

Some friends are seasons, brief and fast, But you are one made true to last. A cherished light, a faithful view, I am so lucky knowing you.

Warm Light

Like evening lamps on winter streets, Your care is warm, your kindness meets The tired parts I hide from view, Then turns them bright with something true.

True Companion

A true companion walks with grace, Not rushing steps, not losing pace. Through joy and tears, through what we do, I trust this road because of you.

Little Moments

We build a life from little things, From talks and smiles and what each day brings. Those little moments, bright and new, Are now my favorites, thanks to you.

Thank You Note

If words were flowers, I would send A field of thanks from start to end. For all your care, for all you do, This simple note says I treasure you.

Steady Star

When nights feel long and skies are wide, Your friendship stands right by my side. A steady star, a faithful hue, I never feel alone with you.

Soft Courage

You show me courage can be kind, Both brave in heart and clear in mind. A gentle strength in all you do, The world is better made by you.

Forever Thankful

For every laugh and every cheer, For every time you stayed right here, I keep one truth in every view, Forever, I am thankful for you.

Good Days Ahead

With friends like you the road feels clear, The future brighter, never fear. No matter what the seasons do, I know good days are built with you.

My Favorite Chapter

In all the pages life can write, Your friendship is my favorite light. A chapter warm, sincere, and true, My story shines because of you.
